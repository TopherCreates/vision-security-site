Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 26, 2026

Vision Security LLC ("Vision Security," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit visionsecurity.net, use our services, or otherwise interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you submit a form on this website, request a quote, schedule service, sign up for our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, property address, and the contents of your message or request.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and other usage data through cookies and similar technologies. We use Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request
  • To send you appointment reminders, service notifications, and post-service follow-up communications
  • To send marketing communications (only with your consent), including email and SMS
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. SMS / Text Message Communications

By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent box on any form, you consent to receive text messages from Vision Security. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for assistance. Your consent is not a condition of purchase.

4. Email Marketing

If you opt in to marketing emails, you can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in any marketing email. Transactional emails (appointment confirmations, service updates) are not marketing communications.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (CRM, communications platform, payment processor) under confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement or regulators when legally required
  • In the context of a business transfer or merger (with notice to you)

6. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise these rights, email adminsupport@visionsecurity.net.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and deliver targeted communications. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though parts of the site may not function correctly.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted with an updated effective date.
