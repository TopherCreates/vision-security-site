Our Story

Founded in 2004.
Still owned by the founders.

Jennifer and Ed Johnson founded Vision Security in 2004 to bring serious life safety to a valley that was, at the time, served only by underqualified independents and out-of-town corporate operators. Twenty-one years later, the company protects more than 1,300 active accounts across Aspen, Vail, and Glenwood Springs.

What hasn't changed: the owners answer their own phones. The technicians know every property by name. Every system gets walked before a proposal is written. Every service call gets followed up two weeks later. And every NICET certification is paid for, scheduled, and tied to compensation by the company.

What has changed: a fully NICET-certified technician team. Beta-testing relationships with Alarm.com. Top Dealer status with DSC across the Western United States. Active referrals from local fire marshals and AHJ officials. The One Vision Program — a consolidated compliance solution no competitor in the market offers.
