Thanks. In production, this form would post to Scale.

This is the prototype submission page. Below is exactly what happens when this form is submitted in production once Scale (white-labeled GoHighLevel) is configured.

Step 1 · 0 sec

Form posts to Scale

HTTPS webhook to the Scale workspace. All fields plus UTM parameters and page source are captured on the lead record.

Step 2 · < 5 sec

Lead created in pipeline

New Lead stage in either the Residential B2C or Commercial B2B pipeline based on the Property Type field.

Step 3 · < 60 sec

Auto-response fires

Branded email confirmation sent. If SMS consent was given, a confirmation SMS sends from the registered 10DLC number.

Step 4 · < 60 sec

Internal alert routes

SMS and email alert to the on-call specialist for that lead type. Emergency or Same-Day requests trigger the hot-lead workflow.

Step 5 · < 1 business hour

Human follow-up

NICET-certified specialist returns the call or message. Lead status updates in Scale as the conversation progresses.

Step 6 · Ongoing

Nurture workflow

If no decision in 7 days, automated educational follow-ups continue at 7, 14, and 30 days until won or lost.

For the Developer

Wiring this form to Scale

In the production build, replace the form's action attribute with the Scale webhook URL provided by the Scale workspace admin. Fields map directly to Scale custom fields. Lead source tag is set per form name. Pipeline routing is configured inside Scale workflows. The standard form fields (name, email, phone, SMS consent, marketing consent) are required; form-specific fields (property type, urgency, etc.) are added per use case. See SITE_SPEC.md Section 4 for the full forms inventory.
